# Call of Duty localization strings

Here is a collection of localization strings from older CoD titles. It's currently just zombies games, as this is what we're here for, other games may or may not come at a later date.

## Encoding

For T4 and T5, the files all have specific encoding you'll have to use while opening and saving them. T6 is all UTF-8 tho.

| Languages | Encoding |
| :---: | :---: |
| English, French, German, Italian, Spanish | Windows-1252 |
| Japanese | SHIFT-JIS |
| Korean | EUC-KR |
| Polish | Windows-1250 |
| Russian | Windows-1251 |

> [!TIP]
> For VSCode, after opening a file, on the bar at the very bootom of the program, you'll find a `Select encoding` button, it'll most likely have `UTF-8` value on it. Click on it, and then press `Reopen with Encoding` and select the encoding from the list above (it'll most likely suggest the correct one).
> For saving, click the same button and press `Save with Encoding`
